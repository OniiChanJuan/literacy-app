Mockup v2 · for approval · June 2026
The new bar

A composition bar, not a plain fill

The old bar just repeated the number as a teal fill — dead weight. The new one shows what the score is made of: external critics, community, and recommend, each as a segment. Today it's all external (one teal segment, with the community/recommend space ghosted to show it's waiting). As you get ratings, the bar visibly fills in and rebalances — so the bar itself becomes an honest at-a-glance signal of how "real" the blend is.

Today — external only
External critics 100% community & recommend activate as ratings come in
Later — once a title has ratings + tags
External 50% Community 35% Recommend 15%
The bar now does real work: it's the visual twin of "what goes into this," and it honestly shows the blend is external-heavy today without needing a sentence to say so. As community fills in, the violet segment grows — you can literally watch the score become more "CrossShelf."
