Design decisions baked in

  • Score scale 0-10 with small teal "CrossShelf" wordmark above the number
  • Horizontal fill bar on Picked for you cards (proportional to score/10)
  • Identity strip compact, full identity behind "View profile →" tap
  • Filter row collapsed into "Filter by type & genre" pill (expands on tap)
  • Cross-shelf gets multi-source rows, each with brief italic "why" description
  • Picked for you cards stay clean (no per-card "why")
  • Editorial section headers — no emojis
  • Small curated rows fit 3 full cards per scroll position (~85-90px wide)
  • Bottom tab navigation: For You / Explore / Library / People
  • "See all →" affordance on smaller rows, right-aligned
  • Serendipity slot uses muted gold border-left, distinct from personalized rows
  • Per-type discovery rows moved from For You to Explore tab
